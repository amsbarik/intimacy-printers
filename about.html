Introduction

Intimacy Printers

We at Intimacy Printers believe in creating perfection and precision to provide you cutting-edge printing services ranging from pre-press, printing to finishing. Intimacy Printers have been supplying quality printing in Bangladesh since 2004. In this document we introduce our people, culture and capabilities that make our company unique.

Intimacy Printers, (A complete printing solutions) is a dream project of Md. Mahabobur Rahman Khan (Imran), who has about 22 years of experiences in the printing industry. Our core exists total of 6000 sft. (Approx.) land area. We already have achieved the certification as a first class press, by the Printing Industries Association of Bangladesh.

We are a manufacturer and not an Agency or Brokerage. We control all aspects of the production and quality of our manufactured products within our workflow and capacity and the range of these is inclusive of Printing, Binding, Di-cutting, Foiling, UV varnishing, Laminating and finishing.

Message from Proprietor

Strong, healthy and authentic business relationships have always been an important pillar in the success of a company. Protecting your business is our business. Because we believe, we are part of your business. Intimacy Printers not only a printer but also a marketer to our clients who understand the sensitive responsibilities perfectly.

Intimacy Printers was founded in 2004 to satisfy the need of local customers. We are able to offer you a broad range of printing services from graphic design and layout to high-speed, high-volume copying in both digital & offset printing.
